How does Slack integrate with Savio?

If your team shares customer feedback on Slack, you can quickly push it to Savio (or have them do it #scaleyourself).

How does it work?

There are two ways to send feedback from Slack to Savio.

1. Feedback from a dedicated channel
After you connect Slack to Savio, you'll specify a public channel where your feedback is posted. When a message is posted there, Savio will ask you (in Slack) whether you want to send the message to Savio.
