Frequently Asked Questions

What is a User?
A user is someone on your team that has a Savio account. You need a Savio account to do things like collect feedback with the Savio Chrome Extension, manage feedback, prioritize features, or close the loop with your customers.

People who send feedback to Savio from Intercom, Slack, or Help Scout do NOT need a Savio account.

How long do I have to try Savio?
You can try Savio for free with one user account for as long as you'd like. If you need more time to try it on either of the paid plans, we're happy to extend your trial. Drop us a line here.

Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We're hosted on AWS, use HTTPS, and have been shipping software for companies like Microsoft and ESPN for 30+ years combined. You should rest assured that this isn't our first rodeo and your data is safe with Savio.
